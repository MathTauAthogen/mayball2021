We warmly welcome people with disabilities.


There is wheelchair access to all areas except the underbar (which is accessed via a flight of stairs), and the senior parlour, which is accessed through a 70cm wide door. The main entertainment is wheelchair accessible. There is basic seating, an accessible toilet, a gender neutral toilet, and a hearing loop. This isn't a BSL interpreter, a designated quiet space, general car parking, or blue badge parking.


For any further accessibility enquires please contact our Operations officer.
